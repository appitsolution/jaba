HOW TO BUY

  • Create a Wallet

    download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

  • Get Some BNB

    have BNB in your wallet to switch to $JABA. If you don’t have any BNB, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

  • Go to PancakeSwap

    connect to Uniswap. Go to app Pancakewap in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $JABA token address into Uniswap, select Jaba, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

  • Switch BNB for $JABA

    switch BNB for $JABA. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
